What is the method for inserting tent poles to form the basic frame of the tent? (10pt)

A) By laying them parallel to each other on either side of the tent
B) By forming a circle with the poles around the tent
C) By crossing them over each other to form an X shape
D) By assembling them into a square frame outside the tent
Most basic tents will have two tent poles that cross each other to form an X that will form the basic frame of the tent. Thus C is correct.

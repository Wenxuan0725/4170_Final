Lay a tarp

Notes

• Place a plastic or vinyl tarp on the ground
• A barrier between the tent and the soil
• Prevent moisture from seeping in
• Ensure the tarp is slightly smaller than the tent's footprint
• Prevents the tarp from extending beyond the tent's edges
• Avoiding water accumulation underneath during rain

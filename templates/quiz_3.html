How should the tarp be sized and arranged in relation to the tent's footprint? (10pt)

A) Larger than the tent to extend the living space
B) Same size as the tent to match its shape perfectly
C) Smaller than the tent, with no part hanging out beyond the tent's edges
D) Cover only the area where the tent's entrance will be
Fold the tarp into the relative shape of the tent, but slightly smaller. You don't want any part of the tarp to hang out beyond the edge of the tent, otherwise it'll collect water underneath in case it rains. Thus C is correct.

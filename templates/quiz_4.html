How do tent poles typically connect for tent assembly? (10pt)

A) With a locking key mechanism
B) Using bungee ropes or by matching numbered sections
C) Magnetically
D) Using adhesive tape
Depending on your particular tent, they may be connected with bungee ropes, or they may be numbered and require that you connect them yourself. Put the tent poles together and lay them across the flat tent. Thus B is correct.

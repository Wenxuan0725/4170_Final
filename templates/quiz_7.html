When selecting a tent for camping in a rainy area, what feature is most important? (10pt)

A) A tent with a lot of windows for ventilation
B) A waterproof rainfly that covers the entire tent
C) A tent that is lightweight and easy to carry
D) Bright colors to improve visibility during the day
Most basic tents will have two tent poles that cross each other to form an X that will form the basic frame of the tent. Thus C is correct.
